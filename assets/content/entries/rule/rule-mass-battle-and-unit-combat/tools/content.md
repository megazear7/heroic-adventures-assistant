# Rule: Mass Battle and Unit Combat

## Scope

Use this rule for converting creature groups into units, resolving unit combat, and running army-scale battles with morale.

## Consolidated Rule

Mass combat groups lower-threat creatures into units (warband/host) with stat and damage modifications, casualty tracking, and bravery checks replacing standard single-creature health pacing.

## Unit Procedure

1. Choose unit size and apply unit modifiers.
2. Determine unit health characteristic from base creature profile.
3. Resolve movement/engagement with formation and facing rules.
4. Resolve attacks as single unit attack/defense rolls.
5. Convert damage into casualties.
6. Trigger bravery checks when casualty thresholds are crossed.

## Morale and Rally

- Units below threshold risk routing on failed bravery checks.
- Leaders/characters can rally with action expenditure.
- Feature flags (Brave, Fearless, Fearful) materially alter battlefield stability.

## Combined Arms Considerations

- Cavalry, shield wall, veteran, skirmisher features shift tactical role.
- Area effects resolve against units with special damage handling.

## Adjudication Notes

- Keep a clean roster sheet for casualties and morale state.
- Resolve facing/flank legality before attack resolution to avoid retroactive edits.
