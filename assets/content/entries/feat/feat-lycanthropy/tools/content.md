# Lycanthropy

Spend a heroic action to transform into a werewolf for the rest of the encounter. Additionally, each time the sun sets, roll a d20. On a roll of a 1 you transform into a werewolf and lose control over your characters actions. While in your werewolf form, you have the stats of a “slayer” monster of your level. Requires 5 scrolls to acquire this feat. Cannot take Vampirism.
