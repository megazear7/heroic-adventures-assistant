# Invisibility

**Type:** major

Target one creature within 10 spaces. They are invisible but still make sounds. This gives them advantage on stealth checks if they are wearing metal armor, or +5 on stealth checks if they are not wearing armor. If they make a melee attack, they may make a stealth vs. agility attack instead of their normal attack. Roll a d4 at the start of every round, if you roll a 1, the spell ends.

Enhance: Increase the size of the die to see if the spell ends by 1, to a maximum of d12

Enhance: Double the number of targets included in the spell

Enhance: Double the range of the spell
