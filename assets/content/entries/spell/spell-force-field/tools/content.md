# Force Field

**Type:** major

Draw a line between spaces that is two space edges long. All adjacent spaces must be within 10 spaces of you. Any creature must make a strength vs. intelligence roll against you in order to move or make a melee attack across the line. Any creature that attempts to move or make a melee attack across the line and fails takes 1d12 damage. At the start of each round roll a d4. If you roll a 1, the spell ends. The force field reaches 10ft into the air.

Enhance: Double the length and height of the force field

Enhance: Increase the damage by 1d12

Enhance: Increase the size of the die to see if the spell ends by 1, to a maximum of d12
