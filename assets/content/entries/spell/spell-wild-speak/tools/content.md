# Wild speak

**Type:** minor

Place your hand on a plant such as a tree or bush and give it a command. This could be to grow in a certain direction, move a branch in a certain way in order to cover a path or hide something that is on the ground, or obscure a doorway. All plants within 1 space follow the command. The plants take 4 minutes to follow the order.

Enhance: Double the range of the spell such that all the plants within the range are affected

Enhance: Reduce the time the plants take to follow the command by half
