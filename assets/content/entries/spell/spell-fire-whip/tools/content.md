# Fire Whip

**Type:** minor

Every time your initiative card comes up you may spend 1 mana to make an intelligence vs. agility attack with a range of 10 that does 1d12 damage and moves the target 6 spaces directly toward you. Once you choose not to make the attack, the spell ends.

Enhance: Add d12 damage

Enhance: Double the range of the spell and move the target an extra 3 spaces
