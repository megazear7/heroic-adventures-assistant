# Jump Lightning

**Type:** minor

Choose a target within 6 spaces and line of sight and make an intelligence vs. willpower attack against the creature. If you hit, the target takes 1d6 damage. Toughness from metal armor is ignored. After each attack performed with this spell, you may make the same attack against another creature within 6 spaces and line of sight of the previously attacked creature. Reduce the damage of each attack by 1d6. Once you have no damage dice left to perform another attack, the spell ends. The spell cannot target the same creature twice.

Enhance: Add 1d6 damage
