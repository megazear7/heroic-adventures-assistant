# Storm of Ice

**Type:** major

Choose a target within 10 spaces and make an intelligence vs. willpower attack against the creature that does 1d6 damage. On each subsequent enemy initiative card, you can spend 1 mana for each activating creature to make this ice strike attack before any creatures activates on that initiative card. You may choose to spend mana to attack 1 or more of the activating creatures. Any creature hit with this spell may not make major movements until the next round. Once you choose not to spend mana to continue the spell, the spell ends.

Enhance: Add 1d6 to the damage of attacks made with this spell
