# Chrono Freeze

**Type:** minor

Time stops momentarily. While time is frozen you may take 1 major action. If any attacks are made while time is frozen, the spell ends immediately, and time resumes as normal.

Enhance: Extend the duration such that you can take an additional major action. Additional actions are lost if attacks are made while time is frozen.

Enhance: Add 1 target within 8 spaces to the spell effect.
