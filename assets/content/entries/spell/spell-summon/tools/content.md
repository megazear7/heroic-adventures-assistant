# Summon

**Type:** major

Target one empty space within 10 spaces and summon into that space a level 1 beast as a bear, cat, snake, spider, wolf, or other creature that the game master allows that lasts until the end of the encounter. The creature has 1 health.

Enhance: The creature has their normal health.

Enhance: The creatures can be 1 level higher

Enhance: Summon 1 additional creature
