# Earth Roots

**Type:** major

Choose 2 targets within 6 spaces. That target is grabbed by roots and cannot move until the end of the round. The target may take a major action to make a strength vs. willpower check to break free of the roots. If they fail to break free, they take 1d4 damage.

Enhance: Double the range

Enhance: Double the number of targets

Enhance: Increase the size of the damage die by 1, to a maximum of d12
