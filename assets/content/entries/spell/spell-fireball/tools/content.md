# Fireball

**Type:** major

Choose a space within 10 spaces. Make an intelligence vs. willpower attack against each creature within 1 space of the target space. If they fail, they take 1d6 damage. If they succeed, they take half damage.

Enhance: Double the range of the spell

Enhance: Increase the radius by 1 space

Enhance: Increase the damage by 1d6. This enhancement cannot be taken more times than the previous enhancement that increases the radius.

Enhance: The damage ignores armor.
