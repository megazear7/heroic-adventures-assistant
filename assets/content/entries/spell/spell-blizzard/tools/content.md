# Blizzard

**Type:** major

Choose a space within 10 spaces, no line of sight required. Until the end of the next round, make the following attack against every enemy creature the first time it activates within 1 space of that space: Intelligence vs. willpower / d6 damage. The spell ends if you move or take damage. Creatures hit by this spell cannot make attacks during the activation in which they were hit.

Enhance: Add d6 damage

Enhance: Add 1 space to the radius of the blizzard

Enhance: Double the range of the spell
