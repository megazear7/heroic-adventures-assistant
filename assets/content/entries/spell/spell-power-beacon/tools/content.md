# Power Beacon

**Type:** minor

Choose a space within 10 spaces to be the beacon. For the rest of the encounter, every time an ally is targeted by an attack while within 2 spaces of the beacon, add a d4 to this spell (after the attack is completed). At any time, you may spend 1 mana to give one or more d4s from this card to yourself or an ally to add to their skill, agility, intelligence, or willpower roll. As a major action you may make an intelligence vs. willpower attack and spend the dice on this card as damage. If you miss this attack, you deal half damage to the target and the spell ends.

Enhance: Double the range of the spell

Enhance: Increase the size of the dice by 1, to a maximum of d12

Enhance: Increase the beacon range by 1
