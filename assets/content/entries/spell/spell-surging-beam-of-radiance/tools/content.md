# Surging Beam of Radiance

**Type:** minor

While this spell is active, on your initiative you may spend one mana to place a d4 on this spell and roll a dice of the same size. If you roll lower than the number of dice on this spell, the spell is cast. When casting the spell, draw a line from the center of your space to the center of a space 10 spaces away. Assign the dice on this spell as damage to the creatures on or near the line as damage that ignores damage reduction. Creatures closer to you must have more dice assigned than creatures further away.

Enhance: Double the range of the spell

Enhance: Increase the size of the dice by one up to a maximum of a d12
