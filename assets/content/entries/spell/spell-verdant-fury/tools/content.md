# Verdant Fury

**Type:** minor

Change the weather in a one-mile radius from where you are standing for one hour. You raise your arms and call out in a chant, forcing the weather to change as you design. The changes in weather are not extreme, but can be anything from sunny with no clouds, cloudy and rainy, or even an intense thunderstorm. You cannot move while the spell is active. This spell can only be cast once per week.

Enhance: Increase the radius by one mile

Enhance: Increase the duration by one mile
