# Fishing

**Category:** Skill

Fishing is the skill of catching aquatic creatures, from mundane fish to magical beasts lurking in enchanted waters. It involves knowledge of bait, tackle, and the habits of elusive prey like merfolk or sea serpents. In a world of adventure, fishing might yield rare ingredients for potions or clues to sunken treasures. This expertise suits patient hunters or seafaring explorers seeking sustenance or secrets from the deep.
