# Climbing

**Category:** Agility

Climbing is the expertise of scaling sheer surfaces, from rugged cliffs to the walls of forgotten castles. Experts use ropes, grappling hooks, or even magic like spider climb spells to ascend with confidence. This skill unlocks high treasures, escapes from danger, or vantage points for combat. In a world of towering peaks and lost ruins, climbing paves the way to adventure.
