# Ambush

**Category:** Tactics

Ambush expertise embodies the art of surprise, deception, and strategic cunning in both combat and exploration. This skill involves planning and executing small-scale operations such as ambushes, feints, and diversions, using the environment to conceal one’s presence or mislead opponents. Characters proficient in Ambush can outmaneuver foes by luring them into traps, striking from hidden vantage points, or coordinating sudden, overwhelming attacks with allies. Whether navigating treacherous dungeons or engaging in guerrilla warfare against a superior force, this expertise is invaluable for adventurers who favor strategy and guile over direct confrontation, turning the element of surprise into a powerful weapon.
