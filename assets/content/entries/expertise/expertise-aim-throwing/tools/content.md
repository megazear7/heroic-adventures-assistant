# Throwing

**Category:** Aim

This expertise measures your proficiency in accurately propelling objects through the air, whether for combat or practical purposes. Throwing also proves valuable in adventurous tasks requiring accurate projection, such as tossing a rope to an ally across a chasm, hurling a grappling hook to scale a crumbling ruin, or even lobbing a torch into a shadowy lair to reveal hidden dangers. A high Throwing expertise reflects exceptional hand-eye coordination and the ability to judge distances and trajectories effectively, making it a versatile skill.
