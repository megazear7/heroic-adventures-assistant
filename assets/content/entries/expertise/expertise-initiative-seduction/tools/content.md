# Seduction

**Category:** Initiative

Seduction is the subtle art of weaving charm and influence through words, glances, and gestures. It’s the skill of turning a stranger’s guard into trust with a well-timed compliment or coaxing a tightly held secret from a wary rival with a disarming smile. Mastery lies in reading the unspoken—sensing desires, fears, and weaknesses—and knowing just when to press or pull back. Yet, it’s a perilous craft; a single misstep can unravel the illusion, sparking anger or betrayal where trust once bloomed. Those who wield seduction walk a fine line, their charisma a quiet blade, sharp and precise.
