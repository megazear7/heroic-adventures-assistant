# Picking Pockets

**Category:** Skill

Picking Pockets is the subtle craft of lifting valuables from unsuspecting targets, whether in bustling marketplaces or shadowy tavern corners. It demands agility and stealth, as a deft hand slips into pouches or cloaks to snatch coins, keys, or enchanted trinkets. In a realm of magic, skilled pickpockets might pilfer spell components or cursed artifacts, risking detection by vigilant guards or mystical alarms. This expertise thrives in daring heists or covert missions where wealth and secrets change hands unseen.
