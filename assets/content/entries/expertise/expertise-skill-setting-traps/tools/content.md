# Setting Traps

**Category:** Skill

Setting Traps is the expertise of crafting snares, pitfalls, and ambushes to ensnare foes or secure lairs. It blends mechanical ingenuity with magical enhancements, from tripwires and pressure plates to enchanted glyphs that freeze or burn intruders. In a world teeming with monsters, traps can guard camps, slow pursuers, or weaken beasts before a fight. This skill demands foresight and cunning, turning the environment into a weapon against the unwary.
