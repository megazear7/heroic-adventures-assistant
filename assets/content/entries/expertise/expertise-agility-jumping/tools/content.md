# Jumping

**Category:** Agility

Jumping is the physical skill of vaulting over obstacles, spanning gaps, or reaching great heights. It’s crucial for crossing treacherous landscapes, fleeing monstrous foes, or accessing hidden perches in ancient ruins. In a fantasy world, magical boots or spells might boost leaps to defy gravity itself. Adventurers rely on this ability to navigate perilous realms with agility and daring.
