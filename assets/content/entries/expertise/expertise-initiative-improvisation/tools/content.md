# Improvisation

**Category:** Initiative

This expertise reflects a character’s knack for thinking on their feet in unpredictable situations, particularly in social or chaotic scenarios. Whether it’s spinning a quick tale to calm a suspicious innkeeper, adapting a plan when a magical ritual goes awry, or distracting a rampaging beast with an unexpected ploy, improvisation ensures adventurers can pivot swiftly and creatively. It’s perfect for those moments when words or wits must replace weapons, making it a vital skill in a world full of surprises.
