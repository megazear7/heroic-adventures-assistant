# Navigation

**Category:** Initiative

Navigation expertise empowers characters to rapidly assess and traverse unfamiliar or treacherous terrain, from cursed swamps to crumbling castle ruins. By reading signs like moss-covered stones, the flicker of distant torchlight, or the eerie howls of unseen creatures, they can chart a course through danger or disarray. This skill shines when time is short—whether escaping a collapsing cavern or guiding allies past a dragon’s lair—relying on sharp instincts and spatial awareness.
