# Cartography

**Category:** Tactics

Cartography is the art of crafting maps, a vital skill for adventurers exploring uncharted lands teeming with monsters and magic. Skilled cartographers produce detailed maps that reveal hidden trails, mark dangerous lairs, and pinpoint enchanted landmarks. This expertise includes knowledge of geography, political borders, and strategic sites for planning ambushes or defenses. In a realm of swords and sorcery, a precise map can guide a party to triumph or steer them clear of doom.
