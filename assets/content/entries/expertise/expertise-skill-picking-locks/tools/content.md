# Picking Locks

**Category:** Skill

Picking Locks is the art of bypassing mechanical and magical barriers, from rusty dungeon padlocks to enchanted vault doors. Skilled lockpickers use deft fingers and specialized tools—like thieves’ picks or enchanted keys—to disable tumblers or unravel wards. In a world of hidden treasures and monster-guarded secrets, this expertise opens paths to forbidden chambers or ancient crypts. Yet, failure risks triggering traps or alerting foes, making it a gamble of precision and nerve.
