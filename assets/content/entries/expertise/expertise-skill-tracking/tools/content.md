# Tracking

**Category:** Skill

This expertise allows characters to follow trails left by creatures or individuals, identifying footprints, broken branches, and other signs of passage. Skilled trackers can determine the number of beings, their size, and even their speed. This is invaluable for hunting monsters, pursuing fugitives, or avoiding ambushes in the wilderness. In a world of magic and adventure, tracking might also reveal traces of enchanted beasts or hidden lairs.
