## Features

### Deflect: Heroic Action

Use this heroic action *after* rolling an agility defense roll. Increase your block to 5 for that attack.

### Team Combat: Feature

Your initiative is treated as being equal to the initiative of the ally with the next highest initiative.

### Skill Versatility: Feature

Gain 3 expertise training.

### Heroism: Feature

At the start of combat, if all of your heroic actions are spent, regain a heroic action. Requires level 5+.

### Tactics: Feature

When you use a major action, an adjacent ally can immediately use a heroic action.

### Adaptability: Feature

Reroll action checks when the roll on the dice is equal to your training in that action check. For example, if you have 3 training in grappling and you roll a 2, you may reroll.

## About Humans

The humans of Heroic Adventures are explorers, builders, and warriors. Some expand into lands unknown and live in remote villages. These folk push the boundaries of civilization. They often cultivate the earth and subdue it to their basic needs of food and shelter. Others discover ancient secrets that might be left behind by the elder races such as elves and dwarves. They incorporate the knowledge of anyone they encounter and bend it to their needs. Their mighty cities with towers reaching to the skies are imposing even to the most aggressive of foes.

In all of their endeavors, humans tend to come into conflict with others. More than this, there are much more terrifying forces at work than the mere misaligned goals of the civilized. Orcs might always be amassing over the next mountain range or beyond the next forest. Goblins are ever on the move and harassing the edges of civilization. More than this, forces of darkness are always seeking to corrupt, gain power, and unleash destruction at the most opportune time.
