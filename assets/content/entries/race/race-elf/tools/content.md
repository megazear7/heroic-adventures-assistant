## Features

### Battle Lore: Heroic Action

Make an intelligence vs. intelligence attack with a weapon. Requires high elf or half elf.

### Woodland Lore: Heroic Action

Make an initiative vs. initiative attack with a weapon. Requires wood elf.

### Woodland Tactics: Heroic Action

Gain 2 block when not using a shield when fighting in a woodland environment. If you are using a shield, increase the provided block by 1. Requires wood elf.

### Swift: Heroic Action

Increase your major movement speed by 1. Requires level 7+ if not wood elf.

### Arcane Lore: Feature

Acquire 2 mystic spells and gain 2 mana. Lose 1 skill. Requires gray elf or high elf.

### Wild Lore: Feature

Acquire 2 nature spells and gain 2 mana. Lose 1 skill. Requires wood elf.

### Essential Power: Feature

Gain 3 mana. Requires high elf or grey elf.

### World Knowledge: Feature

Gain expertise in three different actions.

### Of The World: Feature

Gain expertise in three different actions. Requires half elf.

### Ancient Mending: Heroic action

Provide 1d20 healing to an adjacent target that is not yourself. Requires grey elf, half elf, or high elf and willpower 3+.

## About Elves

The Elves of Heroic Adventurers have a rich, often tragic history. Long ago, they built vast empires across the world's continents, only to see them fall due to calamities unleashed by their ancient foe, Zeerzashra: the Spider Queen. Some elves, the High Elves, retreated to remote regions, preserving powerful magic untouched by the War of the Dead. Rare, war-hardened, and aloof, they judge others harshly.

Others, descended from the Betrayers or in conflict with Elven Lords, left their lands. These Wood Elves became nomadic forest spirits, mastering wilderness skills. Over time, some wanderers formed small communities, becoming Grey Elves — neither noble nor wild, but akin to humans, who prize their magic and ancient wisdom. Grey Elves have even surpassed the magical prowess of their High Elven kin. In some regions, they’ve intermarried with humans and becoming half elves, forging bonds that bring either peace or strife, depending on the locale.
