# Cleric

## Character creation

+4 health

Acquire 2 divine spells

Your mana equals your willpower + half of your character level rounded up.

You may cast spells while wearing medium or heavy armor or while using a shield.

Any time you cast a spell that provides healing, your heal skill is the minimum healing that your spell will provide.

## Advancement

+3 health

Acquire 2 divine spells

## Features

### Inspiration: Heroic Action

Choose an adjacent ally. They may make an attack.

### Leadership: Heroic Action

Choose an ally with more remaining health than you. They may take a heroic action that does not count towards their limit of heroic actions per day.

### Sacrifice: Minor Action

Choose an adjacent ally. If your damage reduction is higher than theirs, they use your damage reduction. When they take damage, half of all the damage they take is applied to your character instead. This continues until you choose to end the effect or until a round ends where you and the target are not adjacent.

### Direction: Minor Action

Make a minor movement and then choose an adjacent ally. They may also make a minor movement.

### Turn Undead: Heroic Action

When casting a spell apply all the healing as damage that ignores damage reduction. All the targets must be undead.

### Greater Turn Undead: Heroic Action

When using Turn Undead, reroll the lowest damage dice. Requires Turn Undead.
