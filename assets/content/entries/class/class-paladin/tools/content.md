# Paladin

## Character creation

+5 health

You may cast spells while wearing medium, heavy armor, or while using a shield.

Any time you provide healing, your willpower is the minimum health provided.

## Advancement

+4 health

## Features

### Arbitrate: Heroic Action

Make a melee willpower vs. willpower attack that does 1d6 + willpower damage armor.

### Sacrifice: Heroic Action

Take damage that ignores armor equal to your willpower. Do the same amount of damage to up to three enemy creatures within 3 spaces.

### Servitude: Heroic Action

Take damage that ignores armor equal to your willpower. Heal the same amount of damage to up to 3 allies within 3 spaces.

### Humble: Heroic Action

Use this heroic action when being attacked. Do not roll a defense roll. You are automatically hit by the attack. Whatever damage you take, do the same damage to up to three enemy creatures within 3 spaces.

### Holy Order: Feature

Acquire 2 divine spells and 2 mana.

### Sacred Mastery: Feature

Acquire 2 divine spells and 2 mana. Requires **Holy Order**.

### Divine Discipline: Feature

Gain 3 mana. Requires Holy Training.
