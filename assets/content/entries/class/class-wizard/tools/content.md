# Wizard

## Character creation

+3 health

Reduce your strength and skill by 1. You cannot choose increases to strength or skill during the Apply Stat Increases step of character creation.

Acquire 2 mystic spells

Your mana equals your intelligence + your character level

In order to cast spells, a wizard must wield a staff or a wand. If you choose a staff, you can only cast spells while wielding a staff and you gain +2 mana. If you choose a wand, you can only cast spells while using a wand and you gain +1 intelligence. A wand provides +4 initiative when equipped as a primary weapon and requires that nothing is equipped in your off hand in order to cast spells. Gain a wand or staff for free.

You can modify the result of a mage’s guild roll up or down by 1.

## Advancement

+2 health

Acquire 2 mystic spells

## Features

### Power: Heroic Action

Cast a spell and apply a single enhancement at no mana cost.

### Quick Cast: Heroic Action

When casting a major spell, apply the effect immediately.

### Combat casting: Feature

You may wield a staff or wand one handed as a secondary weapon and cast spells even when having another weapon equipped as a primary weapon. Do not draw opportunity attacks when preparing and casting spells.

### Warrior Casting: Feature

Cast spells even while you are not equipped with a staff or wand.

### Armored Casting: Feature

Cast spells even while wearing medium armor.

### Greater Armored Casting: Feature

Cast spells even while wearing heavy armor or a shield. Requires **Armored Casting**.

### Spell practice: Feature

Acquire 2 mystic spells.

### Source: Feature

Gain 3 mana.

### Greater Source: Feature

Gain 3 mana. Requires **Source**.

### Guild Apprentice: Feature

Acquire 1 mystic spell. You may spend 1 hour to make a roll on the mages guild table without being at the mage’s guild.

### Archmage: Feature

Acquire 1 mystic spell. You may modify the result of a mage’s guild roll up or down by 3. Requires **Scholar** and intelligence 5+.
