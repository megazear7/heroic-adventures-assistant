# Priest

## Character creation

+3 health

Acquire 2 divine spells

Reduce your strength and skill by 1. You cannot choose increases to strength or skill during the Apply Stat Increases step of character creation.

Any time you cast a spell that provides healing, your heal skill is the minimum healing that your spell will provide.

Your mana equals your willpower + your character level

## Advancement

+2 health

Acquire 2 divine spells

## Features

### Orison: Heroic Action

Any other character with a prepared spell may immediately apply the spell effect.

### Inspiring Presence: Heroic Action

Choose a character within line of sight. They may take a heroic action that does not count towards their limit of heroic actions per day.

### Benevolence: Heroic Action

Any ally within line of sight that is within melee range of an enemy may make a major move without drawing opportunity attacks. They must not end their movement adjacent to an enemy.

### Vespers: Feature

Add d6 to the healing of any spell you cast that already heals.

### Ex Nihilo: Feature

Add 4 to the range of all spells. Any spell that has a range of adjacent instead has a range of 4.
