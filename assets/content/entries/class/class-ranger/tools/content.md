# Ranger

## Character creation

+5 health

Each time you target a creature with an attack, that creature is now your prey, and you gain 1 hunting point, up to a maximum of 3. If you attack a different target before the original target is killed, then prior to making the attack, your hunting points are reduced to 0 and the new target is now your prey.

## Advancement

+4 health

## Features

### Finish the Hunt: Heroic Action

Make an initiative vs. initiative attack against your prey and add damage to the attack equal to the number of hunting points you have.

### Ploy of the Ranger: Heroic Action

Make an initiative vs. initiative attack against your prey and add to your attack roll equal to the number of hunting points you have.

### Kill Shot: Feature

If you make an attack and kill your prey, make another attack against a new target.

### Stalk the Prey: Feature

Increase your maximum hunting points equal to your initiative.

### Ranger Resolve: Heroic Action

Gain temporary health equal to your initiative + your current hunting points.

### Animal Companion: Feature

Choose an animal companion: dog, wolf, mountain lion, panther, or an equivalent animal approved by your game master. Your pet has stats equal to a minion creature profile of your character level except that it always acts on initiative 1. If your pet ever dies, you may spend 1 week of dedicated in-game time acquiring a new pet of your choice from the below list. You and the game master can use this time to roleplay how you come about acquiring a new pet.

### Claw and Arrow: Feature

Your animal companion gains damage against your prey equal to your hunting points. Requires Animal Companion.

### Ranger Bond: Feature

Gain 1 hunting point when your animal companion attacks your prey. Requires Animal Companion.

### Companions Fury: Feature

Your animal companion has stats equal to a soldier creature profile. It still activates on the initiative 1. Requires Animal Companion
