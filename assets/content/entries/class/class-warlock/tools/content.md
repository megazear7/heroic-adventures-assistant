# Warlock

## Character creation

+3 health

Reduce your strength and skill by 1. You cannot choose increases to strength or skill during the Apply Stat Increases step of character creation.

Acquire 2 mystic spells

You can cast each spell that you have acquired one time per day. When you cast it, roll your hex dice (d4) and spend that much mana on the spell. Take the same number as damage that cannot be reduced. When rolling hex dice, the rolls explode, so if you roll the maximum value, you roll the dice again and add the results. You do this until you do not roll the maximum value.

## Advancement

+2 health

Acquire 1 mystic spell

## Features

### Unleashed Anger: Heroic Action

Cast a spell and add 1 to your hex dice.

### Controlled Tempest: Feature

When casting a spell, reduce the damage you take by half.

### Arcane Greed: Feature

When casting a spell, you may reroll 1s on your hex dice.

### Greater Hex: Feature

Increase your hex dice to 1d6. Requires level 5+.

### Infernal Hex: Feature

Increase your hex dice to 1d8. Requires Greater Hex and level 13+.

### Warlock Training: Feature

Acquire 1 mystic spell.
