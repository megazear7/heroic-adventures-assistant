# Overconfident

**Roll:** 9
**Stat Effect:** -1 tactics

This character struts about, certain their every idea is genius, dismissing warnings as peasant nonsense. They’ll lead a hunting party into a swamp, convinced they know a shortcut, only to get everyone lost. Their unshakable pride blinds them to flaws in their schemes. Humility could save them, but they’d never admit it.
