# Weak Grip

**Roll:** 7
**Stat Effect:** -1 aim

This character’s hands lack the strength to hold a bow taut or hurl a dagger with force, resulting in limp shots that barely reach the target. They’ve dropped more throwing knives than they’ve thrown, much to the amusement of onlookers. Even gripping a mug takes effort some days. Their aim suffers from their feeble grasp.
