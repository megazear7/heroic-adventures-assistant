# Gluttonous

**Roll:** 3
**Stat Effect:** -1 agility

This character can’t resist a second (or fifth) helping of roast boar or honeyed bread, leaving them perpetually stuffed and sluggish. Their belt groans under the strain of their indulgences and dodging a farmer’s angry goose is a sweaty ordeal. Festivals are their downfall, as they waddle away from the feast table. Graceful movement is a distant dream.
