# Malnourished

**Roll:** 17
**Stat Effect:** -1 strength

Thin as a reed from scant meals or a picky palate, this character lacks the brawn to heft a plow or haul water. They shiver through winters, too weak to keep up with sturdier folk. A hearty stew might help, but they’d rather nibble than feast. Strength eludes their hollow frame.
