# Stiff Jointed

**Roll:** 4
**Stat Effect:** -1 agility

Years of sitting by the hearth or old injuries from a cart mishap have left this character’s joints creaky and stubborn. They groan like an old oak when rising from a chair, and a quick sidestep is out of the question. Village children giggle as they hobble after a runaway piglet. Flexibility is a fond memory for them.
