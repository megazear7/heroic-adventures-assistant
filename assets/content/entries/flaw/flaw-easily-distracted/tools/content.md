# Easily Distracted

**Roll:** 7
**Stat Effect:** -1 aim

A fluttering bird, a shouting merchant, or even a shiny pebble can pull this character’s focus mid-shot, sending arrows veering into the void. They’ll stop to admire a cloud while nocking an arrow, forgetting the target entirely. Archery contests are a lost cause—they’re too busy daydreaming. Concentration is their eternal foe.
