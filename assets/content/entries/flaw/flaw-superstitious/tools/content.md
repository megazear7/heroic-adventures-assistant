# Superstitious

**Roll:** 12
**Stat Effect:** -1 intelligence

This character jumps at black cats, spills salt like it’s poison, and sees doom in every crow’s caw, driving everyone mad with their fretting. They’ll refuse to leave the house on a “cursed” day, clutching charms against imaginary hexes. Reason takes a backseat to wild fears. Magic unnerves them most of all.
