# Gullible

**Roll:** 11
**Stat Effect:** -1 intelligence

This character swallows every tall tale—buying “dragon tears” from a shady peddler or bowing to a goat they think’s a cursed prince. They’re the village mark, easily duped by charlatans and pranksters alike. Skepticism is a stranger to them, and it shows. Their trusting nature clouds their judgment.
