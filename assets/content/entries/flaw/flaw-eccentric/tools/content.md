# Eccentric

**Roll:** 3
**Stat Effect:** -1 skill

This character insists on bizarre methods, like stirring soup with a sword or weaving baskets upside down, baffling anyone trying to teach them proper technique. Their odd habits make learning standard skills a chore, as they’d rather invent their own quirky ways. Villagers find their antics amusing until they ruin a communal project. It’s not stupidity—just a very strange perspective.
