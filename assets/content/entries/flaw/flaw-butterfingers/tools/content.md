# Butterfingers

**Roll:** 1
**Stat Effect:** -1 skill

No matter how hard they try, this character can’t hold onto anything for long—spoons, quills, or even a flagon of ale slip from their grasp with alarming regularity. They’ve lost more coins down wells and into gutters than most folks earn in a week. Friends have learned not to trust them with fragile heirlooms. It’s a miracle they haven’t dropped their own boots yet.
