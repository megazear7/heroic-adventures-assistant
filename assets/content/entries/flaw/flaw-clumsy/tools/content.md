# Clumsy

**Roll:** 1
**Stat Effect:** -1 skill

This character often trips over their own feet, drops things, or fumbles simple tasks like threading a needle or tying a knot. Their lack of dexterity turns even a quiet tavern evening into a minor disaster as they knock over mugs or stumble into furniture. In a village, they’re known as the one who can’t carry eggs without breaking them. It’s not malice—just a complete lack of finesse.
