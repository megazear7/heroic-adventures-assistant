# Shaky Hands

**Roll:** 6
**Stat Effect:** -1 aim

This character’s hands tremble like leaves in a storm, making it impossible to draw a bowstring steady or toss a stone on target. They’ve accidentally pelted a friend with a wild throw more than once, earning wary glances. At the village fair, their darts land in the dirt—or someone’s hat. Precision is a cruel joke to them.
