# Heavy Handed

**Roll:** 2
**Stat Effect:** -1 skill

Subtlety escapes this character—they pound dough flat when baking, snap quills when writing, and shatter pottery with a casual grip. Tasks requiring a gentle touch, like mending a cloak or carving a wooden spoon, end in ruin. Neighbors whisper that they once broke a door by knocking too hard. They mean well, but finesse isn’t their forte.
