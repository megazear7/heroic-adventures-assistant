# Lead Footed

**Roll:** 4
**Stat Effect:** -1 agility

This character trudges along as if their boots were forged from iron, leaving deep prints in the mud and waking half the village with their stomping. Sneaking past a sleeping dog—or a bandit—is a lost cause. They blame the cobbler, but it’s their own plodding nature at fault. Speed and stealth are not in their repertoire.
