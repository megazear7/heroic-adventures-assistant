# Overly Cautious

**Roll:** 5
**Stat Effect:** -1 agility

This character moves like they’re expecting a trap under every cobblestone, tiptoeing through life with agonizing slowness. They check each step thrice, fearful of slipping on a wet floor or twisting an ankle in a rut. While others dart about, they lag behind, muttering about unseen dangers. Their wariness sacrifices any chance at nimbleness.
