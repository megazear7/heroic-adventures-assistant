# Audacious

**Roll:** 10
**Stat Effect:** -1 tactics

This character loves a grand, reckless gamble—storming the miller’s barn to catch a thief instead of setting a quiet trap. They scoff at caution, preferring bold moves that dazzle or flop spectacularly. Villagers admire their nerve until it backfires. Subtlety and strategy take a backseat to flair.
