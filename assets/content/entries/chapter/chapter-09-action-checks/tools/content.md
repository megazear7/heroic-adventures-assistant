Action checks

At various times your character will attempt an action that the other rules in the game do not cover. After the player describes the action, the game master. The game master will then choose which character statistic or action check to use.

## Difficulties

Trivial: 6

Easy: 10

Moderate: 14

Difficult: 18

Very difficult: 22

Extremely difficult: 26

Once the game master has chosen which attribute or action check should be used along with the difficulty, the player rolls a d20 and adds the appropriate attribute. If the player has expertise in the action check, they also add the level of their expertise. If their total meets or exceeds the difficulty, the action succeeds, otherwise, it fails. If the difficulty is an apposed roll, then the target of the action rolls a d20 and adds the appropriate attribute or action check expertise in the same manner. In this case, the character taking the action must exceed the target’s roll.

## Expertise

On your character sheet, circle in the bubbles next to the actions that you have expertise in. You can only circle in a bubble with a number equal to or lower than your level. You gain expertise when you increase in level.
