# Cutlass

**Standard Cost:** 8 coins
**Artisan Cost:** 225 coins
**Mastercrafted Cost:** 1,375 coins

#### Medium blade \| One handed \| Melee \| 1d6 damage \| Range 1 \| +2 initiative

Reaction: If you have not yet taken a major action this round, take a major action after being hit by a melee attack. This action counts as your major action for this round.
