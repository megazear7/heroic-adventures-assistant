# Hand axe

**Standard Cost:** 5 coins
**Artisan Cost:** 200 coins
**Mastercrafted Cost:** 1,250 coins

#### Small axe \| One handed \| Melee \| 1d4 damage \| Range 1 \| +1 initiative

Hand axes may act as a ranged weapon that does 2d4 damage and have range 10. Hand axes can be stored as a consumable. After throwing a hand axe, you may immediately draw another hand axe if one is available as a consumable. You may recover these hand axes after the encounter.
