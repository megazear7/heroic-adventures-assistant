# Mace

**Standard Cost:** 10 coins
**Artisan Cost:** 250 coins
**Mastercrafted Cost:** 1,500 coins

#### Medium blunt \| One handed \| Melee \| 1d6 damage \| Range 1 \| +1 initiative

If the face value of all damage dice are less than the toughness of the target’s armor, ignore the creatures armor.

Bonus Action: Make an attack against a target. If you miss, you may make another attack against a different creature.
