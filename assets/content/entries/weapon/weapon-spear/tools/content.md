# Spear

**Standard Cost:** 5 coins
**Artisan Cost:** 200 coins
**Mastercrafted Cost:** 1,250 coins

#### Medium polearm \| One handed \| Melee \| 1d6 damage \| Range 2 \| +1 initiative

You may attack through allies. Spears may act as a ranged weapon that does 2d6 damage and have range 10. Spears can be stored as a consumable. After throwing a spear, you may immediately draw another spear if one is available as a consumable. You may recover these spears after the encounter.
