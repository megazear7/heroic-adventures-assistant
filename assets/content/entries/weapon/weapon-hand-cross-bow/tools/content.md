# Hand cross bow

**Standard Cost:** 15
**Artisan Cost:** 300 coins
**Mastercrafted Cost:** 1,750 coins

#### Small ranged \| One handed \| 2d4 damage \| Range 20 \| +2 initiative

After attacking with a hand cross bow while duel wielding you must spend one minor action without moving to reload this weapon before you can attack with this weapon again.
