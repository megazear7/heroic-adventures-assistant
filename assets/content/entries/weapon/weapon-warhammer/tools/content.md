# Warhammer

**Standard Cost:** 15 coins
**Artisan Cost:** 300 coins
**Mastercrafted Cost:** 1,750 coins

#### Medium blunt \| One handed \| Melee \| 1d6 damage \| Range 1 \| +0 initiative

If the face value of all damage dice are less than the toughness of the target’s armor, ignore the creatures armor.

Bonus Action: Bonus attacks made with warhammers ignore shields.
