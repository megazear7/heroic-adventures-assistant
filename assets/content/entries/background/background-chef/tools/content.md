# Chef

**Roll:** 2
**Stat Effect:** +1 skill
**Bonus:** 2+B

Your culinary talents have fed everyone from peasants to princes, honed over years in bustling kitchens. Chopping, slicing, and juggling pots under pressure have made your hands deft and your movements precise. You’ve mastered recipes from across the realm, each dish a testament to your skill with a blade—whether it’s a cleaver or a dagger. A good meal, you believe, can win hearts as surely as a sword wins battles.
