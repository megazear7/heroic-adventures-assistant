# Cultist

**Roll:** 14
**Stat Effect:** +1 willpower
**Bonus:** 2+B

Bound to a secretive order, you’ve faced rituals that test mind and soul. Devotion to a strange power—be it god, spirit, or something darker—drives you forward. You’ve stared into the abyss and held firm, your resolve unshaken by fear or doubt. Faith fuels your unbreakable spirit.
