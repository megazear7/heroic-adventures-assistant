# Veteran

**Roll:** 1
**Stat Effect:** +1 skill
**Bonus:** 4

You have served in a military campaign, honing your combat skills through countless battles. Your experience has taught you how to read opponents and adapt to various combat situations. Scars and tales of valor mark your past, earning you respect or fear among those who know your history. Whether you fought for a lord or your own survival, your hands are steady and your instincts sharp.
