# Gladiator

**Roll:** 2
**Stat Effect:** +1 skill
**Bonus:** 2+B

You’ve battled in dusty arenas, entertaining bloodthirsty crowds with your mastery of weapons and combat styles. Each fight was a test of skill, forcing you to learn new techniques and exploit weaknesses in your foes. The roar of the crowd still echoes in your ears, a reminder of your time as both prisoner and performer. Your flair for showmanship is matched only by your deadly precision.
