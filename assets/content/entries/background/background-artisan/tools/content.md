# Artisan

**Roll:** 8
**Stat Effect:** +1 aim
**Bonus:** 2+B

Your hands craft beauty—be it painting, carving, or weaving—with a meticulous touch. A steady grip and sharp focus guide your tools, placing every stroke or cut just so. What you create is art, but the skill behind it translates to any target you set your sights on. Detail is your domain.
