# Dancer

**Roll:** 5
**Stat Effect:** +1 agility
**Bonus:** 4

Your feet glide across floors in taverns and grand halls, each step a note in a living melody. Years of training have given you poise and control, turning movement into beauty. Whether performing for coin or celebration, your agility shines through every twirl and leap. Grace, you’ve found, can be as deadly as it is enchanting.
