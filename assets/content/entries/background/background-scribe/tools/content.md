# Scribe

**Roll:** 11
**Stat Effect:** +1 intelligence
**Bonus:** 4

Copying ancient texts and deciphering faded runes has steeped you in magical lore. Your quill dances across parchment, recording spells and secrets few understand. Years bent over desks have given you insight into the arcane, even if you rarely cast it yourself. Knowledge is power, and you hold it close.
