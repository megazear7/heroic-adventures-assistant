# Slinger

**Roll:** 6
**Stat Effect:** +1 aim
**Bonus:** 2+B

With a simple sling, you’ve turned stones into deadly projectiles, striking targets from afar. Practice has taught you to judge wind and distance, making each shot a calculation. From herding sheep to fending off bandits, your aim has kept you alive. Humble as it seems, your weapon is an extension of your will.
