# Librarian

**Roll:** 12
**Stat Effect:** +1 intelligence
**Bonus:** 2+B

Surrounded by dusty tomes, you’ve unearthed fragments of arcane truth amid endless pages. You catalog and study, piecing together spells and histories lost to time. Your mind is a repository of knowledge, sharp and attuned to magic’s echoes. Silence and ink are your gateways to power.
