# Strategist

**Roll:** 8
**Stat Effect:** +1 tactics
**Bonus:** 2+B

You’ve pored over maps and chronicles, studying battles won and lost through cunning. Your mind weaves plans like a spider spins webs, anticipating moves before they’re made. Whether advising a lord or leading a village militia, your foresight turns chaos into order. Knowledge is your weapon, wielded with precision.
