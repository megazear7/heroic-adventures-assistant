# Sailor

**Roll:** 3
**Stat Effect:** +1 agility
**Bonus:** 4

Life at sea has made you nimble, climbing rigging and dodging waves on storm-tossed decks. You’ve hauled sails, navigated cramped quarters, and leaped from ship to shore with ease. The ocean’s unpredictability has sharpened your reflexes, and the creak of wood underfoot feels like home. Your sea legs serve you well on land or in a fight.
