# Monk

**Roll:** 13
**Stat Effect:** +1 willpower
**Bonus:** 4

A life of prayer and asceticism has tempered your spirit and body into unyielding steel. You’ve fasted, meditated, and faced inner demons in silent cloisters. Physical hardship and mental discipline are your daily bread, forging a will few can match. Serenity hides your stubborn strength.
