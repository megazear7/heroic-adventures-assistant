# Mercenary

**Roll:** 1
**Stat Effect:** +1 skill
**Bonus:** 4

As a soldier for hire, you’ve fought in numerous conflicts across the land for the promise of gold. Your life has been a series of skirmishes, sieges, and shady deals, teaching you to think quickly and wield your weapon with precision. You’ve learned to size up employers and enemies alike, making you adept in chaotic situations. The clink of coin is as familiar to you as the clash of steel.
