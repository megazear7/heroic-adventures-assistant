# Acrobat

**Roll:** 4
**Stat Effect:** +1 agility
**Bonus:** 2+B

As a traveling performer, you’ve captivated audiences with flips, leaps, and daring balancing acts. Your body bends and twists with grace, a skill honed through years of practice on tightropes and tumbling mats. Crowds marvel at your feats, unaware of how your agility aids you in less theatrical pursuits. Every step is a performance, every move deliberate.
