# Apprentice

**Roll:** 13
**Stat Effect:** +1 intelligence
**Bonus:** 4

Serving a wizard, you’ve swept floors and stirred cauldrons while absorbing the basics of magic. Lessons were harsh, but they drilled spells and theories into your mind. You’ve glimpsed the unseen, enough to wield a spark of arcane might. Your training is just the beginning of a greater path.
