# Thief

**Roll:** 4
**Stat Effect:** +1 agility
**Bonus:** 2+B

You’ve survived by slipping through shadows, picking pockets, and scaling walls under moonless skies. Quick fingers and silent steps are your trade, honed in marketplaces and noble estates alike. The law calls you a criminal, but you see it as an art form requiring finesse. Danger is just another dance you’ve mastered.
