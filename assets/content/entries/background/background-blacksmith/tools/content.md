# Blacksmith

**Roll:** 3
**Stat Effect:** +1 skill
**Bonus:** 4

Years at the forge have shaped you as much as the metal you craft into weapons, armor, and tools. Hammering iron requires a keen eye and steady hand, skills that translate beyond the anvil. You understand the weight and balance of what you create, giving you an edge in wielding or repairing it. The heat of the fire and the rhythm of the hammer are your oldest companions.
