# Hunter

**Roll:** 5
**Stat Effect:** +1 agility
**Bonus:** 4

Tracking game through tangled woods and open plains has made you swift and light-footed. You’ve dodged branches, scaled cliffs, and pounced on prey with practiced ease. The wild has been your teacher, sharpening your senses and speed over countless hunts. A bow or spear feels like an extension of your agile frame.
