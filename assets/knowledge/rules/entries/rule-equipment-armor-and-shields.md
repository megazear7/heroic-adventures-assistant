# Rule: Equipment, Armor, and Shields

## Scope

Use this rule for armor/shield mechanics, grades, enchantments, and defensive loadout tradeoffs.

## Consolidated Rule

Armor converts mobility/defense tradeoff into toughness. Shields add block and initiative penalties. Grades and magical versions scale item power and economy cost.

## Armor Core Mechanics

- Armor grants toughness and typically reduces agility effectiveness.
- Toughness directly reduces incoming damage unless bypassed.
- Armor can be partially bypassed by effects that ignore armor.

## Shield Core Mechanics

- Shields provide block values and initiative penalties by size.
- Block checks occur on unmodified defense die face values.
- “Ignore shields” bypasses shield-derived block, not all non-shield defensive effects.

## Progression and Economy

- Standard/artisan/mastercrafted tiers influence affordability and power curve.
- Enchantments and magic variants should track campaign level pacing.

## Adjudication Notes

- Apply shield and armor effects separately.
- Resolve block first where applicable, then opposed roll, then damage/toughness.
