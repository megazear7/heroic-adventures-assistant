Generate 5 Heroic Adventures campaign hooks for:
${input:setup:Party level, setting, and campaign tone}

Requirements:
- Prioritize `generate-adventure`, `build-npc`, and `rule-gm-worldbuilding-and-npcs` guidance.
- Keep hooks compatible with encounter pacing expectations for the stated party level.
- Do not invent unsupported canon; if a mechanic is uncertain, label it as a suggested ruling.
- Keep hooks concise and table-ready.

Each hook must include:
1. Hook title (short).
2. Immediate trigger.
3. Stakes if ignored.
4. One key NPC or faction pressure.
5. Likely first encounter type and objective.
6. One escalation option if players delay.

After the 5 hooks, add:
- Best fit recommendation (1 hook + why).
- Quick reskin notes (names/faction swap) for fast reuse.
